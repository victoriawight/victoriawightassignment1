Bull Trout

Scientific Name: Salvelinus confluentus

Detailed Status: Threatened

Arctic grayling are a colourful fish that live in the Athabasca, Peace and Hay rivers. They live in boreal and foothill streams. They are known to be easy to catch that has led to their decline. Other reasons for their decline is that they are sensitive to warm water and they have a high natural mortality rate.
