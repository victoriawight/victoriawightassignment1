Woodland Caribou

Scientific Name: Rangifer tarandus caribou

Detailed Status: Threatened

Population: 8167

Caribou are found throughout Canada’s north and are defined by the boreal and southern mountain populations. There are limited herds remaining and one Banff heard died off in 2009. The herds don’t tend to merge and stay as separate populations. They are in decline due to human activity and climate change
